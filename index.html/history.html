I have been editing videos since 2018, I started off on a moblie editing app called InShot.

At this point I was making quick short simple videos, but I wanted to learn and get more complex with editing.

In late 2018/early 2019 I moved over to Capcut which at the time gave me more freedom to do more complex stuff for free.

Then in late 2019 I decided too move over to desktop, I used the family computer to edit but it couldn't handle heavy video editing.

When I moved over to desktop, I used a free editing software call DaVinci Resolve to edit videos.

Switching over to desktop was a whole new experience, the softwares between desktop and moblie were completely different.

moblie's editing softwares are more user friendly and I had to start from zero when learning how to use desktop

I studied it for days, watching youtube videos, experimenting, learning from other editors, etc.

In late 2020 I wanted to do heavier video editing so I bought a PC that is capable of editing and playing video games.

Around mid 2021 is when I started editing videos for other people and getting paid for it.

Through editing I made a bunch of new friends, connections, and it helped me learn a lot.

A buddy of mine shared his Adobe account with me, allowing me to use Premiere Pro

So I used DaVinci Resolve until mid 2023 then switching over to Premiere Pro.

Although the Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve were kind of similar it still took a while to get used to Premiere Pro

In February 2024, I wanted to make my own Content with a channel Im pretty serious about and I ended up actually making my channel.

Editing videos for other people and making my own content on my free time.

In September 2024, I built myself a whole new computer with better specs than my old computer but this time I focused more on the gaming aspects.

I ended up getting a Macbook for editng in November 2025 and thats what I usually use now to edit videos on.

Till this day I still use Premiere Pro but now I have my own account that I pay for myself.

Yup that's my history with editing videos!! :)
